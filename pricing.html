FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need any technical knowledge?

None at all. We handle 100% of the technical work. You simply tell us your business goals and we build everything for you.

How does the payment process work?

We require a 50% deposit to begin work. The remaining 50% is due when your project is delivered and approved. We accept credit card and bank transfer.

How long does a project take?

Starter websites: 5–7 days. Growth packages: 7–14 days. Pro packages: 14–21 days. We'll give you an exact timeline on your discovery call.

What if I want changes after delivery?

All projects include one round of revisions. After that, changes are covered by a monthly retainer plan, or billed at our standard rate.

Can I cancel a monthly retainer?

Yes — retainers are month-to-month with no long-term contract. Cancel anytime with 30 days' notice.
