Who We Are

Built for Main Street, Not Wall Street

SmithDev Labs LLC was founded on a simple belief: small businesses deserve the same powerful AI tools that large corporations use — without needing a six-figure tech budget or an in-house development team.

We work exclusively with small and medium-sized businesses — the plumbers, dentists, restaurants, contractors, and service providers who are the backbone of every community. These are people who are great at what they do, but don't have time to become AI experts.

That's where we come in. We handle all of the technology so you can focus on running your business. From a stunning website that generates leads around the clock, to AI chatbots that answer customers at 2am, to automation systems that eliminate hours of manual work — SmithDev Labs delivers it all, done for you.
